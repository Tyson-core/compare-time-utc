## Privacy Policy

Thank you for using our Chrome extension designed to aid developers. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it. This privacy policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our extension.

## Key Points:

- No Data Collection: Our extension does not collect any personal data from its users. We do not gather information about your browsing activities, personal identifiers, or any other form of data.
- No Data Transmission: Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data transmission to any external servers or third parties. All functionalities of our extension operate locally on your device.
- Purpose: The sole purpose of our extension is to assist developers by providing the necessary tools and functionalities. We ensure that our extension remains focused on this purpose without infringing on your privacy.

## Data Security:

- We prioritize your privacy and security. Since our extension does not collect or transmit any data, the risk of data breaches or unauthorized access is eliminated.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy:

- We may update our privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document with the updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.
